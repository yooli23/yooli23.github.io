I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at Columbia University, where I am a member of the [Natural Language Processing group](http://www.cs.columbia.edu/nlp/people.cgi) and am fortunate to be advised by [Prof. Zhou Yu](https://www.cs.columbia.edu/~zhouyu/).

My research interests lie in **interactive dialogue systems** — in particular, knowledge-grounded dialogue, dialogue summarization, and the use of dialogue systems for language learning.

For more details, please see my [full CV]({{ '/files/Yu_Li_s_CV.pdf' | relative_url }}) (PDF).
